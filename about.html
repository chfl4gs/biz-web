About V5 Connect Sdn Bhd

V5 Connect began its gourmet journey in 2005, and has since been established as a Chinese food chain in Malaysia. With centralized kitchen, quality of its products are strictly maintained.

Its signature Chinese specialities are imported and also freshly produced in the local factory and consist of over 100 varieties of mouthwatering Chinese sausages and ham.
